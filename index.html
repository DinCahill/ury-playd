URY playd

University Radio York Player Daemon

Welcome

URY playd is a minimalist audio player written in C++11. It leverages libsox, PortAudio, and libuv to decode audio, play it to any output device, and take commands via a simple, line-based TCP protocol.

playd is part of the BAPS3 project at University Radio York.
