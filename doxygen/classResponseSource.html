Detailed Description

Abstract helper class for sources of responses.

A ResponseSource can both 'push' responses to a registered ResponseSink and be 'polled' from outside to dump its current response to an external ResponseSink. For example, PlayerPosition 'pushes' its position every few milliseconds to the outside world, to keep the client aware of the time, but is also 'polled' on a new client connection so that the client immediately gets the current position on connect.
