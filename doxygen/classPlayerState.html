Member Enumeration Documentation

enum PlayerState::State : std::uint8_t
strong

Enumeration of states that the player can be in.

The player is effectively a finite-state machine whose behaviour at any given time is dictated by the current state, which is represented by an instance of State.

Note
If you're adding new states here, update PlayerState::STRINGS.
See also
PlayerState::STRINGS
Enumerator
STARTING 

The player has just initialised.

EJECTED 

The player has no song loaded.

STOPPED 

The player has a song loaded and not playing.

PLAYING 

The player has a song loaded and playing.

QUITTING 

The player is about to terminate.
